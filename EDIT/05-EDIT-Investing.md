# Investing Page (/investing) — Editing Guide

## Main content
- What it is: your investing philosophy page — headline, the 3 story paragraphs, the philosophy bullet list, and the career goal note at the bottom.
- Controlled by: `content/investing.yaml`.
- Safe to change: `headline`, `subhead`, all paragraphs in `body`, all bullets in `philosophy`, and `career_note`.

## IMPORTANT — hard rules for this page
- **Never add specific dollar amounts or account balances or returns** (e.g. "$50,000" or "up 30%"). Keep it about skill, process, and discipline — not numbers. This rule is written right at the top of the file as a reminder.
- **Don't say "self-taught"** — frame your learning as mentored/guided by family, since that's the consistent story across the whole site.
